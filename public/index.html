Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need technical knowledge to use DoctorPod?

No technical knowledge required! DoctorPod is designed to be user-friendly with an intuitive interface. We also provide complete onboarding support and training materials to get you started.

Can I migrate my existing patient data?

Yes! We offer data migration assistance for Enterprise customers. For smaller clinics, you can manually add patient records as you go, or contact our support team for guidance.

After 30 days, you can choose to upgrade to a paid plan to continue using all features. If you don't upgrade, your data will be preserved for 30 days, giving you time to decide. No surprise charges!

Is patient data secure and HIPAA compliant?

Yes! You can customize your prescription header with clinic logo, doctor details, and registration numbers. The prescription format follows medical standards while allowing personalization.
